# Refund FAQ

## When will I receive my refund?

After NimbusPay approves a refund, the time for funds to appear on the user's side depends on the bank. Most refunds arrive within 5 to 10 business days.

## I requested a refund but do not see the money

Please check:

- whether the refund was approved
- whether the original payment card is still active
- whether the bank is still processing the reversal

If the refund was approved more than 10 business days ago, support should investigate the case manually.

## Can a refund be sent to another card?

No. Refunds are typically returned to the original payment method used for the purchase.
