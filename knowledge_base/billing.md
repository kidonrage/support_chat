# Billing and subscription notes

If a card payment is declined, the most common reasons are issuer rejection, expired card data, or a temporary fraud check.

Recommended steps:

1. Ask the user to confirm card expiration date, CVC, and billing address.
2. Suggest retrying with the same card once and then with another card if available.
3. If the subscription is in past_due state, explain that access may be limited until payment succeeds.
4. If the user wants to cancel, direct them to the subscription settings page and explain when cancellation becomes effective.

Do not claim that funds were captured unless the billing context explicitly confirms it.
