# Subscription FAQ

## How do I cancel a subscription?

To cancel a subscription:

1. Open Settings.
2. Go to Subscriptions.
3. Select the active subscription.
4. Choose Cancel subscription.

Cancellation stops future renewals. It does not refund the current billing period automatically.

## I cancelled but I still have access

This is expected in most cases. When a subscription is cancelled, access usually remains available until the end of the already paid billing period.

## Why did my subscription renew automatically?

Subscriptions renew automatically by default unless they were cancelled before the renewal date.

## I cannot find the cancel button

The cancel button may be unavailable if:

- the subscription is already cancelled
- the subscription was purchased through a partner channel
- the account currently has no active renewable subscription
