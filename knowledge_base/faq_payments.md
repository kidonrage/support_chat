# Payments FAQ

## Why was my payment declined?

A payment can be declined for several common reasons:

- insufficient funds
- the bank rejected the transaction
- the card has expired
- 3D Secure verification was not completed
- the card is temporarily blocked for online payments

NimbusPay does not always receive the exact decline reason from the issuing bank.

## My payment is pending

A pending payment means that the payment request was created but final confirmation has not yet been received. In most cases, pending payments resolve automatically within 30 minutes.

If the payment remains pending for more than 24 hours, support should review the payment status manually.

## I was charged twice

A duplicated charge can happen when:

- the first payment attempt timed out but was later captured
- the user retried payment before the previous attempt fully resolved
- the issuing bank displayed a temporary duplicate authorization

Temporary duplicate authorizations usually disappear automatically within 3 to 7 business days.

## My saved card disappeared

Saved cards may disappear from the wallet if:

- the card expired
- the bank reissued the card with new details
- the user removed the card on another device
- a security review required the card token to be invalidated
